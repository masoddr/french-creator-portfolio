About Me.

Born in Paris, I moved to the U.S. with a dream—to combine my passion for soccer and storytelling with higher education. My journey began as a Division 1 student-athlete at Chicago State University, where I developed a strong work ethic, teamwork, and leadership skills. I later had the opportunity to live from my passion for soccer and gained professional experiences in the U.S. These are qualities I now bring to both my videography business and my pursuit of a tech sales career.
